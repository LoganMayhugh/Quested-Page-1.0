Welcome to Quested!

Welcome to Quested, an adventure strategy RPG game with a wide variety of possible surprises to use against opponents, lots of different gamemodes, and a character roster of 130 characters! With lots of levels to try out, and even more possibilities of team layouts, there can be many ways to get through whatever you would be trying to get through, whether it is an annual event, quest, PvP match, or a boss battle at the end of every level. Can you become the next questmaster?
