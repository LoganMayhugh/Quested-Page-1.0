Characters

Common Earth Character Larrow

Larrow is the character each player starts off with, and is an Earth Honorbound Archer. Being a Common character, Larrow is very easy to upgrade, and can help low level players out. Having the Earth element, Larrow deals extra damage to Electric element characters, but deals less damage to Magic element characters. The name of Larrow comes from "Land" and "Arrow".

Larrow's Moves and Abilities

Larrow's Basic Move: Shoot
Shoot is a physical ranged attack that hits the target enemy with low damage.

Larrow's Special Move: Sharp Shooter
Sharp Shooter is three physical ranged attacks that hit the target enemy with medium damage for each shot, and has a 30% chance to purge a buff for each shot. This means it is possible to lose three buffs when hit, or don't lose any. This attack has a cooldown of four turns.

Larrow's First Extra Move: Defensive Stance
Defensive Stance allows the user to have 2.5x defense, but 0.5x attack. This does wear off after three turns, or when Offensive Stance gets used. This move has a cooldown of three turns.

Larrow's Second Extra Move: Offensive Stance
Offensive Stance allows the user to have 2.5x attack, but 0.5x defense. This does wear off after three turns, or when Defensive Stance gets used. This move has a cooldown of three turns.

Larrow's Final Extra Move: Piercing Barrage
Piercing Barrage is a physical ranged attack that hits the target enemy with high damage, ignores armor and increased defense, and purges increased defenses after the attack. This move has a cooldown of six turns.

Uncommon Fire Character Meteorite

Meteorite is an uncommon character found in the Cosmic Summon Portal, and is a Fire Barbarian Rogue. Being an Uncommon character, Meteorite is fairly easy to upgrade, and can help low-mid level players out. Having the Fire element, Meteorite deals extra damage to Metal element characters, but deals less damage to Water element characters.

Meteorite's Moves and Abilities

Meteorite's Basic Move: Meteor Punch
Meteor Punch is a fire melee attack that hits the target enemy with low damage, and a 10% chance to burn on attack.

Meteorite's Special Move: Meteor Crash
Meteor Crash is five or six fire ranged attacks that hit random target enemies with medium damage for each hit, and has a 30% chance to burn for each hit. This deals extra damage to burning targets, and it is possible to have one enemy be hit by all meteors, or each enemy gets hit once. This move has a cooldown of five turns.

Meteorite's First Extra Move: Fireball
Fireball is a fire ranged attack that hits the target enemy with low-medium damage, applies burn, and gives self Rage. This move has a cooldown of four turns.

Meteorite's Second Extra Move: Heatwave
Heatwave is a fire ranged attack that hits all enemies with low damage, deals extra damage to burning enemies, and will kill enemies hit and left at less than 10% health. This move has a cooldown of four turns.

Meteorite's Final Extra Move: Meteor Armor
Meteor Armor grants self 1.3x defense, and has burning skin and touch. This wears off after four turns. This move has a cooldown of five turns.

Rare Water Character Sapphire

Sapphire is an rare character found in the Jewel Summon Portal, and is a Water Construct Caster. Being a Rare character, Sapphire is not easy to upgrade, but not hard. Also, she can help mid level players out. Having the Water element, Sapphire deals extra damage to Fire element characters, but deals less damage to Electric element characters.

Sapphire's Moves and Abilities

Sapphire's Basic Move: Starlight Strike
Starlight Strike is a cosmic ranged attack that hits the target enemy with low damage, and a 10% chance to daze on attack.

Sapphire's Special Move: Flowic Sapphire
Flowic Sapphire is a physical ranged attack that hits all enemies with medium damage, and deals 1.3x damage for each buff on each ally. So if the attack deals 100 damage regularly, and an ally has Regeneration, the damage is boosted to 130 to each enemy. But if that same ally had Attack Up, or a different ally had it, the damage is boosted to 160 damage instead, bacause there are two buffs. After the attack, the target enemy will have dazed. This move has a cooldown to five turns.

Sapphire's First Extra Move: Swift
Swift is a cosmic ranged attack that hits the target enemy with low-medium damage, and provokes the target to the ally with the most defense until the target attacks. Provoke forces the provoked to use their next attack on the provoker. This move has a cooldown of three turns.

Sapphire's Second Extra Move: Power Gem
Power Gem repairs self 10% of its max health, and grants self attack up and two energy. This move has a cooldown of five turns.

Sapphire's Final Extra Move: Sapphire Slings
Sapphire Slings is two cosmic ranged attacks on each target enemy with low damage per hit, and steals a random buff from a random enemy, even if they avoided the attack. This move has a cooldown of three turns.

Epic Magic Character Shade Bladex

Shade Bladex is an epic character found in the Competitive Events, and is a Magic Assassin Rogue. Being an Epic character, Shade Bladex is fairly difficult to upgrade, but can help high level players out. Having the Magic element, Shade Bladex deals extra damage to Earth element characters, but deals less damage to Metal element characters.

Shade Bladex's Moves and Abilities

Shade Bladex's Basic Move: Shadow Swing
Shadow Swing is a physical melee attack that hits the target enemy with low damage, and a 30% chance to give Mystical, which take more damage from Magic attacks.

Shade Bladex's Special Move: Sinister Shadow Strike
Sinister Shadow Strike grants attack up for all allies, then deals a physical melee attack on the target enemy with high damage. If there is any other assassin ally, the damage deals 10% more. This move has a cooldown of five turns.

Shade Bladex's First Extra Move: Assassinate
Assassinate is a physical melee attack on the target enemy with medium damage, and ignores taunt or provoke. If the attack kills, Assassinate is used again on the enemy with the lowest health. The chain does not stop until Assassinate hits without killing. This move has a cooldown of four turns.

Shade Bladex's Second Extra Move: Shadow Surikens
Shadow Surikens is three or four physical ranged attacks that hits random enemies with low damage, and purges a buff on each hit. If the enemy is hit without a buff, this deals 10% more damage for each assassin ally on the user's team, excluding Shade Bladex. An enemy can be hit by all four surikens, or three different enemies get hit. This move has a cooldown of four turns.

Shade Bladex's Final Extra Move: Life Stealing Sneak
Life Stealing Sneak is a physical melee attack on the target enemy with medium damage, lifesteals 90% of damage dealt, and is unavoidable. This move has a cooldown of three turns.

Legendary Electric Character Nova

Nova is a legendary character found in the high ranks in Competitive Events, and is an Electric Undead Caster. Being a Legendary character, Nova is very difficult to upgrade, and can help high level players out greatly. Having the Electric element, Nova deals extra damage to Water element characters, but deals less damage to Earth element characters.

Nova's Moves and Abilities

Nova's Basic Move: Sun Shine
Sun Shine is a sun ranged attack that hits the target enemy with low damage, and a 20% chance to apply Sunburn. Sunburn has the character take extra damage from Sun attacks.

Nova's Special Move: Supernova
Supernova is a sun ranged attack that hit all enemies with very high damage. This move has a cooldown of seven turns.

Nova's First Extra Move: Sun Rays
Sun Rays is three sun ranged attacks that hit the target enemy with low-medium damage each, and 30% chance to apply Sunburned. This move has a cooldown of four turns.

Nova's Second Extra Move: Solar Protection
Solar Protection grants self Sunburning Skin (melee attackers might get this effect when hitting the user) and will take 0.75x damage to sunburned enemies. This move lasts for three turns and has a cooldown of four turns.

Nova's Final Extra Move: Solar Barrage
Solar Barrage is a sun ranged attack on all enemies with medium damage, and a 30% chance to apply Sunburn to each enemy hit. This move has a cooldown of four turns.

Mythical Metal Character Anvil

Anvil is a mythical character found only through campaign, and is a Metal Construct Tank. Being an Mythical character, Anvil is extremely difficult to upgrade, but can be a threat to go against. Having the Metal element, Anvil deals extra damage to Magic element characters, but deals less damage to Fire element characters.

Anvil's Moves and Abilities

Anvil's Basic Move: Metallic Punch
Metallic Punch is a physical melee attack that hits the target enemy with low damage, and a 50% chance to slow the enemy hit.

Anvil's Special Move: Raw Material
Raw Material is a physical melee attack on all enemies with medium damage. This attack has no cooldown, but the more it gets used, it deals 5% less damage for the rest of the battle.

Anvil's First Extra Move: Toughened Taunt
Toughened Taunt grants the user Taunt (forces enemies to target the user instead of other allies), defense up, deathproof (prevents the user from dying on the first hit that would kill them, and the user would instead be a one hp), and 10% chance to steal a random buff from melee attackers. This move has a cooldown of six turns.

Anvil's Second Extra Move: Titanium Armor
Titanium Armor multiplies the user's defense by 3 for three turns. This move has a cooldown of four turns.

Anvil's Final Extra Move: Guardian Shield
Guardian Shield grants all allies (excluding self) defense up and a shield (prevents first attack on user from doing anything, then shield breaks). This move has a cooldown of four turns.
